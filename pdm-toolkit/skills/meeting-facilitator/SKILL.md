# Skill: meeting-facilitator

## Purpose
Facilitate delivery meetings with a clear structure, focused outcomes, and documented decisions — so meetings feel purposeful, not wasteful.

## When to Use
- Any recurring delivery meeting (sync, planning, review)
- High-stakes meetings where decisions must be made
- When meetings are running long without outcomes

## Framework: Delivery Meeting Facilitation

### Before the Meeting
- [ ] Define: what decision or outcome does this meeting need to produce?
- [ ] Invite only people needed for that decision
- [ ] Share agenda 24 hours in advance
- [ ] Ensure decision-makers are present

### During the Meeting

**Opening (2 min)**
> "Today's goal is [specific outcome]. We have [X] minutes. Let's start."

**Timekeeping**
- Assign a timekeeper
- Give 2-minute warnings before each section ends

**Parking Lot**
- Create a "parking lot" for off-topic items
- Capture them visibly, address after or in a follow-up

**Decision Protocol**
When a decision is needed:
1. State the decision needed (not the discussion topic)
2. Present options (max 3)
3. Get input from key voices
4. Make the decision
5. Record it: "Decision: [X]. Owner: [Name]. Date: [Date]."

**Closing (3 min)**
> "Before we close — actions: [read them out]. Decisions: [read them out]. Next meeting: [date]."

### Meeting Health Signals
🟢 Healthy: Decisions made, actions assigned, ended on time
🟡 At risk: Discussion without conclusion, same people talking
🔴 Broken: No decisions, no actions, running over, wrong people in room

## Output Format
1. Meeting agenda template
2. Decision and action log (filled during meeting)
3. Follow-up summary (ready to send post-meeting)
4. Suggested next command: `/decision-log` or `/status-update`
