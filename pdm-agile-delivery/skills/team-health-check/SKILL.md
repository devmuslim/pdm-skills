# Skill: team-health-check

## Purpose
Assess team health across motivation, communication, delivery process, and psychological safety — and generate targeted improvement actions.

## When to Use
- Quarterly team review
- When team morale appears low
- After a difficult sprint or release

## Framework: Team Health Check (Traffic Light Model)

### 8 Health Dimensions

For each dimension, team members vote: 🟢 Great / 🟡 OK / 🔴 Struggling

| # | Dimension | Question |
|---|-----------|---------|
| 1 | **Collaboration** | Do we work well together and support each other? |
| 2 | **Clarity** | Do we understand our goals and priorities? |
| 3 | **Process** | Do our ways of working help rather than hinder us? |
| 4 | **Delivery** | Are we shipping quality work at a good pace? |
| 5 | **Learning** | Are we growing our skills and learning from mistakes? |
| 6 | **Fun** | Do we enjoy our work and each other's company? |
| 7 | **Psychological Safety** | Can we speak up without fear of blame? |
| 8 | **Stakeholder Trust** | Do stakeholders trust and support the team? |

### Step 1 — Run the Survey
5-minute anonymous survey. Each person rates each dimension.

### Step 2 — Aggregate Results

| Dimension | 🟢 % | 🟡 % | 🔴 % | Trend vs Last Check |
|-----------|------|------|------|---------------------|
| Collaboration | 80% | 20% | 0% | ↗ |
| Clarity | 40% | 40% | 20% | → |
| Psychological Safety | 30% | 30% | 40% | ↘ |

### Step 3 — Focus Areas
Address the bottom 2 red dimensions first. One concrete action per dimension.

## Output Format
1. Health check survey (ready to run)
2. Results table with trend
3. Top 2 focus areas with recommended actions
4. Suggested next command: `/working-agreements` or `/retrospective`
