# Skill: vendor-communication

## Purpose
Generate professional, clear communication templates for common vendor interactions — status requests, delay responses, breach notices, and escalations.

## When to Use
- Chasing a vendor for an overdue deliverable
- Responding to a vendor delay notification
- Sending a formal SLA breach notice
- Escalating a vendor issue internally

## Templates

### Template 1 — Status Request
Subject: [Project Name] — Delivery Status Request

> Dear [Vendor Contact],
>
> I'm writing to request a status update on [deliverable] which is scheduled for [date].
>
> Could you please confirm:
> 1. Current completion status (% complete)
> 2. Confidence in the [date] delivery date
> 3. Any risks or blockers we should be aware of
>
> We need this update by [date] to complete our release planning.
>
> Thank you,
> [Your name]

---

### Template 2 — Delay Response (Vendor Notified Us)
Subject: Re: [Project Name] — Delay Acknowledgment and Next Steps

> Dear [Vendor Contact],
>
> Thank you for notifying us of the delay to [deliverable]. We understand the new estimated date is [new date].
>
> To help us manage the downstream impact, we need:
> 1. Root cause of the delay (brief)
> 2. Confirmed delivery date with high confidence
> 3. What support, if any, you need from our team
>
> Please respond by [date]. We will need to escalate internally if we do not receive a confirmed date by then.
>
> [Your name]

---

### Template 3 — SLA Breach Notice
Subject: Formal Notice — SLA Breach: [Service Name]

> Dear [Vendor Contact / Account Manager],
>
> This is formal notice that [Vendor Name] has breached the following SLA commitments in the period [date range]:
>
> - [Metric]: Committed [X%] / Actual [Y%]
> - [Metric]: Committed [X hours] / Actual [Y hours]
>
> Per our agreement (Section X, Clause Y), we request:
> 1. A formal root cause analysis within 5 business days
> 2. A remediation plan with committed improvement targets
> 3. Applicable SLA credits for the breach period
>
> Please respond to this notice by [date].
>
> [Your name] | [Title]

---

### Template 4 — Internal Escalation (Vendor Risk)
Subject: Vendor Risk Escalation — [Vendor Name] — Action Required

> Hi [Manager Name],
>
> I'm escalating a vendor risk that requires your visibility and potential action.
>
> Vendor: [Name]
> Issue: [Brief description]
> Impact: [What this affects — release date, feature, etc.]
> Deadline: Decision needed by [date]
>
> Options:
> A) [Option A] — Risk/Benefit
> B) [Option B] — Risk/Benefit
>
> Recommended: [Your recommendation]
> Ask: [Specific ask from manager]
>
> [Your name]

## Output Format
1. Relevant template(s) filled with provided context
2. Tone calibration notes if needed
3. Suggested next command: `/sla-review` or `/escalation-framework`
